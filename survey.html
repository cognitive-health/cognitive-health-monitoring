Keystroke Health Monitor

An innovative app for monitoring cognitive health through keystroke dynamics.

Monitor Your Cognitive Health

The Keystroke Health Monitor app discreetly tracks your typing behavior. It does not track or score what you type, but rather how you type: analyzing keystroke dynamics like typing speed and error rate. This data is a valuable indicator of cognitive health, allowing for early detection of cognitive decline. Early detection is crucial for slowing the progression and maintaining a higher quality of life.
